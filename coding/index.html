Welcome, I am Keanu Petersen multimedie student

For those who are new, my name is Keanu Petersen I'm 21 years old and currently I am doing my first semester as a Multimedie-design student at UCL University College. My passion for video editing and in general editing started when I was around 14, when I was tasked to edit a short film my group and I had made for a school project and it was here I discovered the magical thing about editing. Being able to change something raw into a masterpiece was truly fascinating and it really opened up my eyes for the world of editing. From that point on I have always wanted to do something related to content creation whether it be video or photos it all sounded good to me. During my years from that point on I have made some small projects in my spare time, exploring the world of content creation and everything it had to offer, which is why I ended up at UCL, because it was here I finally could do what I always had wanted to do, sharpening my abilities even further and becoming greater than I ever could have imagined and finally become a professional in the field of content creation..
